Dual
AI
Assistant
A single AI assistant with two personas — Fraud Analyst and CFO — querying the same underlying data fabric. Every answer links back to transactions, cases, entities, and ERP records with full explainability and traceability.
Safe-guardrails make outputs suitable for investor updates and Board reporting. Variance explanations highlight which drivers — volume, pricing, fraud rate, costs — change each KPI.
  • "Show me recent high-risk transactions over ₹10,000 and estimate their impact on monthly revenue if we block them."
  • "Explain variance in last month's EBITDA vs. forecast, factoring in fraud loss and chargebacks."
  • "Under a conservative growth scenario, how many months of cash runway do we gain if we cut fraud losses by 30%?"
  • "What is the impact of current fraud patterns on cash runway and EBITDA margin?"
  • "If we tighten fraud thresholds, what is the trade-off between reduced fraud losses and conversion drop?"
CFO Assistant — Sample Response
Cutting fraud losses by 30% under the conservative scenario adds approximately 1.4 months of cash runway and improves EBITDA margin by ~0.8pp. Threshold tightening carries an estimated 2.1% conversion risk on high-value merchant flows.
