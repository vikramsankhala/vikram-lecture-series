Sennheiser HD 490 PRO+
Open-Back Studio Monitor

The HD 490 PRO+ delivers exceptional clarity and detail with its extended frequency response of 5Hz–36kHz. Designed for professional studio monitoring, these open-back headphones provide accurate sound reproduction and comfortable extended listening sessions.

The innovative angled drivers and open-back design create a spacious soundstage with precise imaging, making them ideal for critical listening, mixing, and mastering applications. The detachable cables and robust construction ensure longevity in professional environments.

With 130Ω impedance and 110dB SPL sensitivity, these headphones pair perfectly with both dedicated amplifiers and portable devices, offering versatility across various listening scenarios from studio work to mobile enjoyment.

FiiO K5 Pro ESS
Desktop DAC/Amp

The K5 Pro ESS represents the pinnacle of desktop audio conversion, featuring the flagship ES9038Q2M DAC chip. This desktop solution delivers exceptional audio quality with multiple input options including USB, optical, and coaxial connections for maximum versatility.

With 550mW+550mW output power at 32Ω, the K5 Pro ESS easily drives demanding headphones while maintaining pristine audio quality. The hardware volume control and dual gain settings provide precise level adjustment for any headphone impedance.

The comprehensive I/O includes RCA line outputs for connecting to powered monitors, making it a complete desktop audio hub. The ESS Sabre DAC implementation ensures low distortion and high resolution playback up to 32-bit/768kHz.

Portable DAC/Amp

The KA13 revolutionizes portable audio with desktop-grade performance in a compact USB-C dongle format. Featuring the dual Cirrus Logic CS43131 DAC chips, it delivers exceptional sound quality from smartphones, tablets, and laptops.

With 220mW+220mW output power, the KA13 can drive most headphones with authority while maintaining battery efficiency. The built-in hardware volume control and UAC1.0 compatibility ensure broad device support including gaming consoles.

The all-metal construction provides excellent durability and heat dissipation, while the multiple output options (3.5mm single-ended and 4.4mm balanced) offer flexibility for various headphone types and cable configurations.
